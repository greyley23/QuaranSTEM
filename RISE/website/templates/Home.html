Hello everyone and welcome to this website.

This is a project made for me, Juan Avila, in order to participate in RISE FOR THE WORLD; I want this project to be a repository of multiple academic, educative and informative files in order to be of help for those people (especifically teenagers in high school and preparatory) who were affected the most for the poor educational response coming from the schools and educational institutions they belonged in front of COVID-19 Quarantine.

I'll make sure that the information that comes to this website is the most reliable and accurate.
